# Website Redesign Summary

## Overview
Complete modern redesign of Parveen Packaging Industries website with enhanced content and updated business timeline (2017-2026, 9 years).

## Design Changes

### Color Palette
- **New Modern Purple/Blue Gradient Theme**
  - Primary: Deep purple-blue (oklch(0.42 0.19 260))
  - Accent: Vibrant pink-purple (oklch(0.65 0.22 320))
  - Gradients: Multi-color gradients for hero sections
  - Enhanced shadows and depth

### Typography & Spacing
- Increased font sizes for better readability
- Larger headings (5xl-7xl for hero sections)
- Improved line heights and spacing
- Better visual hierarchy

### Components
- Rounded corners increased (0.75rem radius)
- Enhanced card shadows with hover effects
- Modern gradient backgrounds
- Improved button styles with hover animations
- Better mobile responsiveness
